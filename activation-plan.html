Chapter 09 · Activation Plan

90 Days to Pipeline Full

Week by week — from invisible to fully in-market. No overwhelm. Just the right things in the right sequence.

The North Star

By Day 90: CELO has a client-facing website live, a defined ICP and service tiers, a reactivation campaign completed with 2–3 new conversations, a LinkedIn outbound system producing weekly inbound, and 2 referral partner relationships active. That's the foundation for $300K–$600K ARR within 12 months of consistent execution.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–2
Foundation: Get Client-Ready
Days 1–14
Build the client-facing website — minimum viable: homepage, services page, booking page. Careers section moves to /careers. Go live within 2 weeks.
Define 3 service tiers with pricing ranges. Write one-page descriptions for each. These become the services page content AND the outreach materials.
Rebuild LinkedIn company page — new banner, updated description with client-facing positioning, service showcase enabled.
Write one case study — even anonymised. One result, one before/after. This is the most important content asset CELO doesn't have.
Build the warm list — compile all past proposals, churned clients, and warm LinkedIn connections into a spreadsheet. Segment by pool type.
Day 14 Milestone
Website live. Service tiers defined. LinkedIn rebuilt. Case study written. Warm list segmented.
Phase 2 · Weeks 3–4
Reactivation: Wake the Warm List
Days 15–28
Send reactivation sequence to all three warm list pools (Chapter 05 templates). Target: 30–50 outreach messages over 2 weeks.
Begin LinkedIn content — 2 posts per week. Topic mix: outsourcing ROI insights, "a day in the life of a CELO team member," client success stories (anonymised). Build the brand, not just the pitch.
Identify 5 referral partners — web agencies, business coaches, or bookkeepers serving your ICP. Send personal outreach: "We serve the same clients and don't compete — worth a 20-minute call?"
Book at least 3 diagnostic calls from reactivation and LinkedIn. The goal is conversations, not contracts. Every call is research AND a sales opportunity.
Day 28 Milestone
Reactivation sequence complete. 3+ diagnostic calls booked. LinkedIn posting rhythm established. 2 referral partner conversations started.
Phase 3 · Month 2
Engine: Outbound Goes Live
Days 29–60
Launch LinkedIn outbound — 10 connection requests/day to ICP-matched prospects. Begin follow-up sequence with all accepted connections.
Launch cold email — 50 targeted emails per week to sourced ICP contacts. Track reply rate and adjust subject lines and first lines based on data.
Sign first referral partner agreement — formalise the relationship with commission structure. Even a handshake agreement in writing creates accountability.
Close first new retainer client from warm list or outbound. Even a small team engagement ($2,000–$3,000/month) validates the system and creates a new case study.
Day 60 Milestone
Outbound producing 3–5 calls/week. First referral partner signed. First new retainer client closed or in final conversation.
Phase 4 · Month 3
Scale: Compound the Momentum
Days 61–90
Add second case study from the new client closed in Month 2. Real results, specific metrics. Add to website immediately.
Optimise outbound — review LinkedIn and cold email data. Double down on what's generating replies. Kill what isn't. The system is only as good as the iteration.
Activate second and third referral partners — with the first one producing results, the pitch to the next two is easier. Show what happened with partner #1.
Target 3 total retainer clients by Day 90 — at an average of $5K/month that's $15K MRR / $180K ARR run rate. The pipeline to hit this is built in Months 1–2.
Day 90 Milestone
3 active retainer clients. 2 case studies live. Outbound producing consistent pipeline. Referral network active. $15K+ MRR baseline.
