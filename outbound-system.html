Chapter 07 · Outbound System

Building the Client Pipeline

LinkedIn, cold email, and referral partners — three channels, one consistent flow of qualified prospects.

LinkedIn Outbound

Where your ideal clients already are

💼
LinkedIn — Daily Outreach System

Target profile: Founders, COOs, Ops Directors, VPs of Operations at companies with 10–200 employees in US, UK, AU. Industry filters: e-commerce, SaaS, professional services, agencies, healthcare admin.

Daily cadence: 10 connection requests + 5 follow-up messages to pending connections + 2 content posts per week. Consistency over volume.

Profile optimisation first: Before any outreach, CELO's company LinkedIn page and the key team members' profiles must lead with client-facing messaging — not job listings.

Connection Request Message
"Hi [Name] — I work with [founder/ops-led] businesses in [industry] helping them scale their operations with dedicated Philippines-based teams. Came across your profile and thought we might have something worth a conversation. Would love to connect."
Follow-Up After Connecting (Day 3)
"Thanks for connecting, [Name]. Quick question — are operations keeping pace with your growth, or is that becoming a bottleneck? We help companies like yours build reliable remote teams for support, admin, and back office. Happy to share more if it's relevant."
Cold Email System
📧
Cold Email — Targeted & Personal

Source: Apollo.io, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, or manual list-building by ICP criteria. Target 50–100 prospects per week. Use a dedicated sending domain (not the main CELO domain) to protect deliverability.

Sequence: Email 1 (Day 1) → Follow-up 1 (Day 4) → Follow-up 2 (Day 9) → Break-up email (Day 16).

Cold Email #1 — The Hook
Subject: "Quick question about your ops team, [Name]"

"Hi [Name] — most [industry] founders I speak with hit a wall around [revenue/headcount milestone] where the ops load starts to slow the growth down. Is that where you are right now?

We help businesses like [Company] build dedicated, embedded remote teams in the Philippines — customer support, back office, admin — without the cost and delay of local hiring.

Worth a 20-minute call to see if it fits? Happy to share a couple of examples from [industry] first."
Referral Partner Strategy

The channels that send you pre-sold clients

The highest-converting leads in B2B services come from referrals. CELO's ideal referral partners are people who work with the same businesses CELO serves — and who don't compete with outsourcing services.

Web & Digital Agencies
Build websites and apps for SMBs. Their clients often need ongoing support and admin. A warm intro from a trusted agency is worth 10 cold emails. Offer 10–15% referral commission on first 3 months.
Business Coaches & Consultants
Work with founders on growth and operations. Regularly encounter the "I need more people but can't afford it locally" problem. Perfect referral partners — not competitors.
Bookkeepers & Accountants
See exactly when a business is growing faster than its cost structure can handle. A bookkeeper who refers one client a month is worth $60K–$80K ARR in retainers.
HubSpot / Salesforce Partners
Implement CRMs for SMBs that immediately need support and admin help to use them. Natural pipeline overlap — they do the system, CELO runs it.

Referral offer: 10% of first 3 months of retainer revenue for any signed client. Simple, clean, trackable. No complex tiering — just a consistent, reliable commission that makes it worth mentioning CELO to every relevant client.
