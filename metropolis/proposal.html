Proactive Intervention Playbooks
High-risk customers get tailored outreach before they call to cancel — an offer, a resolution, a human touch at the moment that matters most.

02
Forecasting AI

Know what's coming
before it arrives.

The best way to handle a surge in call volume is to not be surprised by it. Forecasting AI uses your historical data, real-time signals, and external context to predict volume hours and days before it hits.

Multi-Variable Forecasting
Day of week, time of day, location, city, local events (sports, concerts, holidays), weather, membership density, and historical baselines — all combined into a single volume prediction.
Issue Type Prediction
Not just how many calls are coming — but what they'll be about. Billing spikes after statement dates. App errors after updates. Event-related disputes after concerts. Category forecasting = smarter agent prep.
Parking Occupancy Modelling
Forecast space availability by location and time window. Operations teams can proactively redirect, communicate, and staff accordingly — before lots fill up and calls flood in.
03
Workforce Management AI

Right people. Right time.
Right cost.

Staffing a call centre reactively is expensive. You either pay for idle agents during quiet periods or fail customers during peaks. Workforce Management AI eliminates both by forecasting staffing needs with 90%+ accuracy — automated, weekly, by shift.

90%+ Forecasting Accuracy
Industry benchmark for AI-driven workforce planning with 12+ months of training data. CELO has 3 years. Accuracy from day one — not after months of calibration.
Automated Weekly Schedules
The system generates staffing recommendations by shift, location, and skill set — reducing the management time spent on manual scheduling from hours to minutes.
Cost Reduction Quantified
Overstaffing is as expensive as understaffing. Optimal scheduling typically reduces labour costs by 15–25% while maintaining or improving service levels.
