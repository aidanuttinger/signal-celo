## SECTION 1: BASE ASSUMPTIONS

### Network Scale
- **Total Locations:** 4,500
- **Average Facility Size:** 850 parking spaces
- **Total Capacity:** 3.825 million spaces
- **Growth Rate:** 15% YoY (industry standard for parking tech)
- **Current Members:** ~1.2M active members

### Revenue Model (Per Member, Per Month)
- **Standard Tier:** $12/month (most common)
- **Premium Tier:** $18/month (15% of members)
- **Enterprise/Corporate Accounts:** $22/month (10% of members)
- **Weighted Average:** $13.10/member/month

### Occupancy & Utilization
- **Average Facility Occupancy:** 68% (ranges 45–95% by market)
- **Average Members per Facility:** ~270 active members
- **Growth per Facility:** 12% new members annually
- **No-Show / Cancellation Rate:** 12% monthly (includes all causes)

### Cost Structure (Per Facility, Per Month)

#### Staffing (Call Center)
- **Average Agents per Facility:** 1.8 FTE (ranges 1–4 by volume)
- **Loaded Cost per Agent:** $24/hour ($3,968/month full-time, all-in)
- **Monthly Staffing Cost:** $7,142/facility = $32.1M fleet-wide

#### Fixed Costs
- **Facility Operations (parking lot, equipment, utilities):** $4,500/month
- **Platform Maintenance & Tech:** $2,200/month
- **Corporate/Admin Overhead (allocat​ed):** $1,800/month
- **Insurance & Compliance:** $1,500/month
- **Total Fixed Costs:** $10,000/month/facility = $45M fleet-wide

#### Variable Costs
- **Payment Processing (2.8% of revenue):** ~$1,180/month/facility
- **Member Communication (SMS, email):** $300/month/facility
- **Churn Replacement Marketing:** $600/month/facility
- **Total Variable:** ~$2,080/month/facility = $9.36M fleet-wide

### Revenue Per Facility (Baseline)
- **Members per Facility:** 270
- **Revenue/Month:** 270 × $13.10 = **$3,537/facility**
- **Fleet Revenue:** 4,500 × $3,537 = **$15.92M monthly = $191M annually**

### Feature 5: Dynamic Pricing Signals
**Objective:** Premium parking tier during peak demand — guaranteed spot, guaranteed time.

#### Demand Profile
- **Peak demand days:** Friday–Saturday (2 days/week)
- **Peak hours per day:** 6 hours (10am–12pm, 5pm–8pm)
- **Peak periods per year:** ~520 (2 days × 52 weeks)

#### Pricing Opportunity
- **Standard parking:** $12/month ($0.40/day)
- **Premium offer during peaks:** +$3 for guaranteed spot
- **Uptake rate:** 20% of peak-time parkers
- **Parkers during peak:** 68% occupancy × 850 spaces × 4,500 facilities × 2 days/week = 5.2M parker-days/week
- **Premium subscribers:** 5.2M × 0.20 × 52 = **54M premium parker-days annually**

#### Revenue Calculation
- **Revenue per premium use:** $3
- **Annual premium parking revenue:** 54M × $3 = **$162M**
- **Gross margin:** 85% (minimal incremental cost)
- **Net revenue:** **$137.7M**

#### Cost
- **Dynamic pricing engine:** -$1.2M (one-time) + $0.3M/year (maintenance)
- **Staff training (one-time):** -$0.4M

#### Challenge & Reality Check
This is aggressive. Real-world constraints:
- **Member resistance:** Price-sensitive members may cancel
- **Churn impact:** Estimated 2–3% membership churn from pricing
- **Churn cost:** 3% × 1.2M members × $235.80 × 0.75 (conservative recovery) = **-$63.8M**
- **Net after churn:** $137.7M - $63.8M = **$73.9M** (still very positive)

#### Conservative Variant (Recommended)
- **Soft launch:** "Recommended" pricing, agents suggest (not enforced)
- **Uptake rate:** 8% (vs 20% forced)
- **Revenue:** 54M × 0.08 × $3 = **$12.96M**
- **Churn risk:** Minimal (no coercion)
- **Net benefit:** **$12M** (after costs)
- **Alternative:** Test with pilot cohort first (500 facilities)

#### Net Impact (Dynamic Pricing — Conservative)
- **Year 1 Revenue (soft launch):** $12M
- **No forced churn:** Sustainable growth
- **Year 2 Revenue (optimize based on Year 1 learning):** $35–50M
- **Long-term:** $120M+ once calibrated

**For today's presentation, recommend:** Pilot with 500 facilities for 60 days, measure churn vs revenue, then scale.
