The Problem

Fitness operators are leaving hundreds of millions on the table.

You're running one of the most operationally complex businesses in the world. Multiple locations. Staff scheduling across peaks and valleys you can't predict. Members churning silently. Payments failing quietly. Each facility operating in isolation, without the data to know what's coming.

The industry has accepted this as normal. Overstaffing to cover worst-case scenarios. Reactive payment recovery that chases members after they've already decided to leave. Scheduling based on gut feel instead of behavioral data. The cost of this operational blindness is enormous — and entirely preventable.

The average 4,500-location network is losing over $375M per year across four compounding inefficiencies. Not because operators aren't trying. Because they've never had the tools to see it happening.

$127.8M
Staffing Overspend
The average facility runs 1.8 FTE call center agents scheduled against worst-case volume. During normal hours, 30% of that capacity sits idle. At $24/hour loaded cost across 4,500 facilities, overstaffing burns $127.8M annually. Every year. Without a single bad outcome to justify it.
$56.7M
Silent Payment Failures
12% monthly cancellation rate across 1.2M members — and a significant portion is payment failures, not genuine cancellations. Members whose cards expire or decline are silently lost before anyone notices. Proactive detection and recovery can reclaim the majority of this revenue before the member even knows there's a problem.
$69.8M
Experience-Driven Churn
Members leave when they feel like a number. Long wait times with no communication. Shift changes that drop context mid-interaction. Generic responses from agents who don't know their history. Each of these micro-failures compounds into churn. The revenue impact of even a 0.5% reduction in monthly churn across 1.2M members is transformational.
$120M+
Untapped Revenue Opportunities
Dynamic pricing based on real-time occupancy. Ancillary services timed to member behavior patterns. Loyalty upgrade pathways triggered by engagement signals. These aren't future concepts — they're revenue channels that exist today in your member data. Without an intelligence layer to surface them, they remain invisible.
